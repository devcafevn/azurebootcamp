Speakers

Hanh Nguyen

Hanh Nguyen has worked in software development for 10+ years. He has focused on web technologies like ASP.NET MVC, ASP.NET CORE, Angular, and specially in cloud computing with Microsoft Azure. Hanh has been certified as an Azure Solutions Architect

With over ten-year experiences in the full life-cycle of the software design process including gathering requirements, writing technical proposal, estimating, developing and maintenance services.
