Speakers

Hanh Nguyen

Hanh Nguyen has worked in software development for 10+ years. He has focused on web technologies like ASP.NET MVC, ASP.NET CORE, Angular, and specially in cloud computing with Microsoft Azure. Hanh has been certified as an Azure Solutions Architect

Thien Nguyen

Thien is Engineering Manager at NashTech, a Microsoft MVP and the creator of SimplCommerce. He is a passionate developer, a mentor and a speaker. His current interests are .NET Core, Azure and Microservices

Phi Huynh

Phi Huynh is currently working as R&D Manager at NashTech, building new skills for the company, help the NashTech’s community up-to-date with latest technology trends (such as Blockchain, Microservices, DevOps, Cloud & AI). He’s also a Software Consultant specializing in cloud-first approach, software architecture and applying DevOps culture to the projects. An Azure Expert to provide excellent services to help enterprises accelerate their cloud adoption. With over ten-year experiences in the full life-cycle of the software design process including gathering requirements, writing technical proposal, estimating, developing and maintenance services.

Duc Lai

Duc is the Supply Chain Data Scientist at Unilever Vietnam. He was the cloud solution architect and took care of DevOps environment for Microservices project on Azure. He loves researching on Microsoft technologies, especially .NET Core, Office 365 and Microsoft Azure. Coming to Global Azure Bootcamp 2019, he would love to share about the Security on Azure Kubernetes Services with open-source tools.

Thang Chung

Thang has over 10-years’ experience in the software development industry. He normally works as a solution architecture on a variety of projects in NashTech. He is a creator and maintainer of awesome-dotnetcore, clean-code-dotnet, coolstore-microservices, cloud-native-kit, and many projects on GitHub.

Dao Vo

Dao Vo is a Cloud Specialist, .NET, Data & Big Data Architect with a strong background of years working in Microsoft and Azure. Dao is working as Azure Ranger at Microsoft.
Dao has also worked with customers in many countries including USA, UK, Australia, Japan, Vietnam, Singapore helping companies adopt the Microsoft cloud.
